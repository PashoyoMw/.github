# Digital Ticketing & Booking Platform

**PashoyoMW** is a digital platform focused on modernizing ticketing, event management, and booking experiences across Malawi and emerging markets.

We build secure, scalable, and user-friendly systems that help organizers manage events efficiently while giving customers a seamless way to discover and access experiences.

---

## What We Do

Pashoyo provides tools for:

* Event discovery and ticket purchasing
* Organizer and event management
* Secure digital ticket validation
* Scalable booking solutions for events and services

Our goal is to simplify operations for businesses while improving access and convenience for users.

---

## Platform Overview

Pashoyo consists of two main components:

* **Web Platform** – Enables event discovery, ticket purchasing, and organizer management
* **Mobile Scanner App** – Handles ticket verification and access control at entry points

These systems work together to ensure reliability, security, and a smooth event experience.
